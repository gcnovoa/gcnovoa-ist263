About Studio Ghibli

Hayao Miyazaki

Hayao Miyazaki is a Japanese animator born January 5th, 1941 in Tokyo, Japan. He began his career in animation in 1963 where he worked at Toei Douga Studio contributing to earlyJapanese classics. In 1982 Miyazaki began work on the manga for ‘Nausicä of the Valley of the wind’, and in two years time it was released as a film. The success of Nausicä led to the creation of Miyazaki’s greatest success Studio Ghibli animation studio. For the past three decades, Miyazaki has animated, written and directed many other animated films along with the co-creator of Studio Ghibli Isao Takahata. Miyazaki is critically acclaimed all over the world with some viewing him as one of the best animators and storytellers.
